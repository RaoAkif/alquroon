Who we are?
E Learningholyquran.com is non-partisan organization; we are not part of any group, Islamic organization or mosques. We welcome all students interested in learning Quran. We are consists of a small team of dedicated workers from Management , Sales and Marketing Team and Islamic Tutors .This team functions with the guidance of many of the Ustadhs for providing quality services to our Muslim brothers and sisters interested to learn the Holy Quran.
How can I register?
You can simply go to ADMISSIONS Then REGISTRATION page. Fill up the required fields. You are not required to give any credit card information for free trail after free trial you will pay a small fee/hadia according to class schedule. We have both Online and Offline payment methods. We do accept Credit Cards and Bank Transfer as well. All registrations and inquiries have a 48 hours response time. We will assist you in the software installation and can start your lesson.
How qualified are your teachers?
We thoroughly check the background of tutors so you don't have to worry who is teaching you and/or your kid. Our teachers teach on full time base and come daily in the office's teaching department. Our teachers are not only selected just for their knowledge, but also for their interpretational skills to gain motivation and encouragement in students to increase the knowledge of the Quran more and more .If they fail we see it as though we ourselves having failed to deliver too.
How online Quran Classes work?
After completion of registration and software installation process, you will be tailored with a well- versed Quran tutor for one-to-one live interactive classes. At an agreed time, teacher and student come online .With help of telephony software, they talk to each other just like a telephone conversation but through computer and a screen sharing software makes them enable to see the same lesson on their computer screen. Teacher can highlight letters and words to the Qur'an to enhance the understanding of the student.
